Background

Emiliano is a teaching co-ordinator at the Edinburgh Medical School. He did an integrated master degree in biological sciences at the Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, Argentina. He did his PhDs at the Universidad Nacional de Córdoba (Argentina) and the Universitat de Lleida (Spain). During his academic career, he speciliased himself in statistical programming and computational biology, with focus on Bayesian Networks as graphical expert systems to facilitate decision making.

Experience

  • Programming languages: R, Python, Shell

  • Data wrangling and pre-processing: integration of databases, feature engineering, data discretization.

  • Data analysis: Bayesian and Social Networks, General and Generalized (Mixed) Models, Machine Learning modelling.

  • Website development

  • Project and partnership management
